Why you can trust it

Every CAT-capable radio and every program was cross-referenced against manuals, Hamlib, RigPix, interface docs, and official app guides — then each value carries its source and a confirmed / corrected / uncertain verdict.

The audit fixed real errors: Yaesu rigs mislabeled with an Icom CI-V address, JRC radios listed at 4800 baud instead of 1200, HID-only rigs marked as having a sound codec.

Still — confirm against your radio's own manual before you wire up. Menu numbers vary by firmware, and your COM-port number is assigned by your PC, so it can't be pre-filled here.
